Analyzing a Sag Span

Activating a Sag Span

A sag span can be activated from the Edit Pane by double clicking the span, or by right clicking and selecting 'Activate'. The span is activated when the font turns bold.

The Analysis

Once a sag span is activated, it will trigger an analysis. The analysis will look at five (5) different cable temperatures. A solution entails:

  • Modeling a catenary based on the cable information and attachment positions.
  • Solving for the selected sagging method.

The analysis usually finishes quickly on modern computer hardware. OnSag also speeds things along by using all of the available CPU cores.

Deactivating a Span

A sag span can be deactivated to clear the analysis results. Right click on the sag span, and select 'Deactivate'.
